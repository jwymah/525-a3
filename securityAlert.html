Campus Community Advisory

UPDATE - Personal Property Thefts

Campus Security would like to inform the campus community that two suspects
connected to the recent rash of personal property thefts in the Taylor Family
Digital Library and Kinesiology Complex have been identified and arrested.


On Saturday April 2, 2016 Campus Security located and identified two suspects
that matched the descriptions provided by members of the campus community.
After interviewing the pair, Calgary Police arrested them for numerous offenses.
Campus Security is continuing to work with Calgary Police to investigate
all of the reported thefts. Additionally, the suspects (both non-students)
were issued indefinite trespass bans from all University of Calgary properties.


Campus Security would like to thank everyone who came forward to provide
information related to this investigation. 

Campus Security would also like to remind everyone to ensure the security
of all personal belongings while on campus. Do not leave valuables, computers
or any personal items unattended and unsecured for any length of time.

A victim of crime is not responsible for the actions of a perpetrator. The safety and security of students, faculty, staff and visitors on campus is of foremost importance to the University of Calgary. While serious crimes on campus are rare, in the event they do occur, Campus Security provides assistance and support to the victims and strives to prevent such incidents from happening in the future. The University of Calgary encourages students, faculty, staff and visitors to please be mindful of personal safety both on and off campus and to be alert when walking alone. Immediately call 9-1-1 to report any serious incidents or suspicious behaviour to the Calgary Police Service. Call the SU Wellness Centre: 403-210-9355, or the Calgary Communities Against Sexual Assault: 403-237-5888, for counselling and support.

This message has been sent to all members of the University of Calgary community.

If you have any information regarding this incident, please contact Campus Security at (403)220-5333 or the Calgary Police Service at (403)266-1234.

If you feel uncomfortable walking alone on campus, contact Campus SAFEWALK at 220-5333 and someone will be sent to accompany you.
