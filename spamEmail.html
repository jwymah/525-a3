3. Review your name and contact information

Deadline: Friday, May 13

Credentials issued by the University of Calgary are prepared using a your full legal name. Initials are not permitted. Any initials in your name will be removed unless legal documentation is provided to the Convocation Office.

Ensure your name in the Student Centre is correct. There is a charge to reprint your degree if information is incorrect. This is also a good time to review your contact information (mailing address, phone number and email) to ensure that it is up-to-date.

To update your legal name, please submit the change of personal information form and a copy of legal documentation.
